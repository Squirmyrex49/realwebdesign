Roman Noodles Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of roman noodles with flavor packet
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 tsp butter
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • 1/4 tsp toasted sesame seeds
  • 1/2 scallion, green part only, thinly sliced on the bias, optional

Preparation

  1. Boil 2 1/2 cups of water
  2. Add noodles and cook for 2 mins
  3. Add flavor packet and stir while cooking for 30 secs
  4. Remove pan from heat and carefully add the egg
  5. Pull noodles over egg and let sit for 1 min to poach
  6. Transfer everything to a serving bowl, add the butter, cheese, and sesame seeds and mix

Optional

  • Garnish with scallions
